AirAsia Group (AAGB) reported headline net loss of RM852m in 3QFY20 . YTD 9MFY20, core net loss stood at RM2.2bn, which accounts for 105% and 94% of our and consensus’ full year expectations respectively. The discrepancy was mainly due to higher-than-expected operational expenses .

Airline revenue for 3QFY20 still declined 87% YoY, bringing cumulative 9MFY20 revenue to RM2.6bn. Non-airline segment saw a marginal LBITDA of RM3.4m in 3QF20, compared to an EBITDA.
